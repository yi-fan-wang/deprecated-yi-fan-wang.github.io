Yifan Wang's Homepage


Welcome to my homepage! I'm Yifan, a PhD student of Chinese university of Hong Kong and a memeber of LIGO and Virgo collaboration.

My research interests include:

* Data analysis of Compact Binary Coalescence (CBC)

* Testing General Relativity with Gravitational Waves (GWs)

* Probing primordial black holes with Gravitational Waves (GWs)

I'm currently devising new statistical methods for the testing general relativity sub-group of LIGO and Virgo to embrace the era when detections of GW events become routine.
